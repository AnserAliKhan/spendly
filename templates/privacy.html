Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our personal expense tracking service. We believe in keeping things simple: we collect only what we need to operate the service, and we never sell your data.

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly's features, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and the password you create when registering.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to log in the app.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with Spendly, such as pages visited and features used, used only to improve the service.
  • Device information — your browser type, operating system, and IP address, used for security and troubleshooting.

We do not collect financial account credentials, bank login details, or any information unrelated to operating Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your Spendly account.
  • To store, display, and let you edit your expense records.
  • To generate the category breakdowns and summaries you request.
  • To send you important account-related notifications.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • To improve Spendly's features, performance, and reliability.

We do not use your expense data to build advertising profiles, and we do not share it with marketers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using modern cryptographic algorithms and are never stored in plain text.

While no system is perfectly secure, we continuously review and strengthen our safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your expense records are retained for as long as your account is active. You may export or permanently delete your data at any time through your account settings, and we will honour deletion requests promptly.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third-party providers to operate the service. These providers may process limited data on our behalf, solely to deliver the functionality you have signed up for:

  • Hosting provider — for storing application data and running the Spendly service.
  • Email provider — for sending account notifications such as password resets and security alerts.
  • Analytics provider — for understanding aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to improve the product.

We select third-party providers that meet strong privacy and security standards. They are only permitted to process data on our documented instructions and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can update most of your account information directly within Spendly's settings. For anything else, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

You may also close your account at any time. When you do, we will delete your personal data unless retention is required by law.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the service or by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We take all privacy inquiries seriously and will do our best to address them promptly.
